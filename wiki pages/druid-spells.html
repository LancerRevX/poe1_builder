1st level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Charm Beast
  • Foe AoE (Only Beasts): Charmed
 Average 8.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 5m 1.25m Radius The druid utilizes his or her innate connection with the natural world to Charm enemy animals in the area of effect.
Dancing Bolts
  • Foe AoE: 15-25 damage
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 3.0m Radius Summons a flurry of thin bolts of lightning, hitting enemies in the area of effect with Shock damage.
Nature's Mark
  • Foe AoE: -10 Deflection
  • Foe AoE: -10 Reflex
 Fast 30.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 10m 2.5m Radius Causes enemies in the area of effect to glow with pale green light, making them easier targets. Affected enemies suffer decreased Deflection and Reflex.
Nature's Vigor
  • Friendly AoE: +40 Endurance
  • Friendly AoE: +15% Maximum Endurance
 Fast 12.0 seconds 1.25m Radius from Caster Draws on the invigorating power of nature, creating a mild regeneration effect and raising Max Endurance on party members.
Sunbeam
  • AoE: 20-35 damage
  • AoE: Blinded
 Average 15.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 1.25m Radius Calls down a shaft of intense sunlight, burning and potentially Blinding those caught in the area of effect.
Talons' Reach
  • Foe AoE: 25-40 damage
 Fast 0.75 sec (Strong) 6m 1.25m Radius Conjures a giant pair of razor-sharp talons, causing Slash damage to all in the area of effect.
Tanglefoot
  • AoE: Hobbled for 4 seconds
 Fast 20.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 10m Ground: 5.0m Radius Rapidly grows a patch of twisted vines that surround and entangle anyone unlucky enough to get caught inside. Characters who set foot inside the area of effect become Hobbled.
Vile Thorns
  • AoE: 26-38 damage (+10 vs )
  • AoE: If successful: Sickened (Poison) for 10 seconds (+10 vs )
 Average 10.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) Length: 3.0m 90° Cone Shoots sharp-thorned vines up through the ground, causing Pierce damage and potentially Sickening anyone in the area of effect.
Winter Wind
  • Foe AoE: 22-32 damage
  • Foe AoE: 2.0m Push
 Average 1.0 sec (Stronger) Length: 8.0m 60° Cone Causes an icy wind of incredible power to arise, pushing back all in the area of effect and inflicting Freeze damage.

2nd level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Autumn's Decay
  • AoE: 15-25 damage
  • AoE: 45 damage over time
 Average 6.0 seconds 0.35 sec (Weak) Length: 5.0m 90° Cone Creates a cone of putrefaction, causing immediate Corrosive damage and then more damage over time to any in the area of effect.
Blizzard
  • AoE: 18-25 damage
  • AoE: -20%  Attack Speed
 Average 10.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 2.5m Radius Conjures a violent storm of ice and snow, slowing inflicting Freeze damage upon any in the area of effect and slowing their Attack Rate.
Burst of Summer Flame
  • AoE: 35-45 damage
 Average 0.2 sec (Weaker) 15m 1.25m Radius Quickly generates an explosive flash of flame, inflicting Burn damage to anyone in the area of effect. Short cast time.
Conjure Lesser Blight
  • Summon 1 of: Small Rain Blight, Small Wind Blight, Small Earth Blight, Small Flame Blight
 Slow 20.0 seconds 6m Gathers a few stray soul fragments, coalescing them into a Lesser Flame, Wind, Rain, or Earth Blight.
Firebrand (ability)
  • Summon Weapon: Firebrand
 Fast 30.0 seconds Caster Forms a powerful sword with a blade of pure fire in the caster's hands that inflicts Burn damage. Switching to another weapon ends the spell.
Hold Beasts
  • Foe AoE (Only Beasts): Paralyzed
 Fast 7.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 1.25m Radius Forces a shrieking cry from the caster's throat, Paralyzing all but the most fearsome of animals.
Insect Swarm
  • Foe AoE: 43.3 damage over time
  • Foe AoE: -10 Concentration
 Average 10.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 1.25m Radius Attracts a swarm of aggressive insects, causing Pierce Damage over time and reducing the concentration of anyone in the area of effect.
Taste of the Hunt
  • Target: 30 Raw damage over time
  • Caster: 100% of Damage restored as Endurance
 Average 10.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) Caster's melee weapon becomes an ideal instrument for hunting, adding continuous Raw Damage to its attack and restoring Endurance to the caster with every hit.
Woodskin
  • Friendly AoE: +6 vs
Fast 15.0 seconds 5m 1.25m Radius Creates a thick layer of bark over allies in the area of effect, affording them a Damage Reduction bonus to Pierce, Slash, Burn, and Shock.

3rd level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Beetle Shell (ability)
  • Friendly Target: 100-pt Stasis Shield
 Fast 30.0 seconds 15m Encases the target in a protective shell, absorbing a fixed amount of damage before shattering, but preventing the target from taking actions (including moving).
Infestation of Maggots
  • Foe AoE: 10 by percentage of lost
 Average 20.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 10m 1.25m Radius Enemies in the area of effect have their skin infested by hordes of burrowing maggots, inflicting Raw damage over time. The more wounded the target, the greater the damage. A target at maximum Endurance takes no damage from the spell.
Nature's Balm
  • Friendly AoE: +60 Endurance over time
 Fast 15.0 seconds 1.25m Radius from Caster Draws on the healing power of nature, creating a rapid regeneration effect on party members.
Purge of Toxins
  • Friendly Target: Immunity: Poison, Disease
 Fast 30.0 seconds 6m Purifies the target of any toxic presence in its body, giving immunity to Poison and Disease effects.
Returning Storm
  • Foe AoE: 20-30 damage ( vs )
  • Foe AoE: If successful: Stunned for 3 seconds ( vs )
 Slow 30.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 5.0m Radius from Caster Brings a powerful storm into being, striking a random single enemy in the area of effect periodically with bolts of lightning that Stun and deal Shock damage.
Spreading Plague
  • Foe Target: Hobbled, Weakened
  • Foe Jump Targets (x5): Hobbled, Weakened
 Average 15.0 seconds 10m + 5m Jump Foe Target + 5 Jump Targets Infects the target with a virulent and debilitating disease, Hobbling and Weakening them before spreading to other enemies.
Stag's Horn
  • Target: 30-55 damage
  • Target: -10 Deflection
  • Target: -10 Reflex
 Average 10.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 10m Conjures an enormous stag horn to attack the target, causing Piercing damage and inflicting a penalty to Deflection and Reflex.
Twin Stones
  • Foe Target: 20 damage (+10 vs )
  • Foe AoE: 25-40 damage (+10 vs )
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) Length: 6.0m + 2.5m Radius Sends two deadly boulders flying out from the druid's position, dealing Crush damage to anyone in their path and exploding on contact with a solid surface, causing the fragments to Pierce anyone nearby.

4th level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Boiling Spray
  • AoE: 40-50 damage
  • AoE: 2.0m Push
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) Length: 6.0m 90° Cone Everyone in the cone area of effect takes Burn damage and is pushed back.
Calling the World's Maw
  • AoE: 25-48 damage (+10 vs )
  • AoE: If successful: Prone (+10 vs )
 Average 7.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 10m 2.5m Radius Summons a jagged rock to stab upward from the ground, dealing Pierce damage to targets and knocking them Prone.
Conjure Blight
  • Summon 1 of: Rain Blight, Wind Blight, Earth Blight, Flame Blight
 Slow 25.0 seconds Gathers a moderate amount of stray soul fragments, coalescing them into a Flame, Wind, Rain, or Earth Blight.
Form of the Delemgan [WM2]
  • Friendly AoE: +8 vs
  • Friendly AoE: +1 Move Speed
  • Friendly AoE: +25 on Disengagement
  • Friendly AoE: Immunity: Stuck
 Fast 20.0 seconds 5m 3.0m Radius Allies gain a protective layer of wooden "skin" and are imbued with characteristics of delemgan, becoming faster, less vulnerable to Disengagement attacks, and immune to the Stuck affliction.
Hail Storm
  • AoE: 30-42 damage
  • AoE: 25% damage as
Average 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 5.0m Radius Calls down a savage storm of sleet and ice chunks, causing Crush and Freeze damage to any in the area of effect.
Moonwell
  • Friendly AoE: +92 Endurance over time
  • Friendly AoE: +10 All
Average 20.0 seconds 6m 2.5m Radius Channels lunar energy into a localized source of power, regenerating the Endurance of allies in the area of effect and granting a minor bonus to all Defenses.
Overwhelming Wave
  • Target: 30-40 damage
  • Target: Stunned
 Average 8.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) Length: 10m Conjures a rolling wave of water that plows through everything in its path, causing Crush damage and the Stun affliction.
Wicked Briars
  • AoE: 10-15 damage (+10 vs )
  • AoE: If successful: Hobbled for 3 seconds (+10 vs )
 Fast 20.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 6m Ground: 2.5m Radius Sprouts thick, thorny vines from the ground, causing Pierce damage to and Hobbling any in the area of effect.

5th level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Cleansing Wind
  • Foe Target: 3.0m Push (+10 vs )
  • Friendly Target: +32 Endurance
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) Length: 10m Creates a rolling cloud of purification, healing the Endurance of allies and pushing back enemies.
Embrace the Earth-Talon
  • AoE: 28-38 damage (+10 vs )
  • AoE: If successful: Petrified (+10 vs )
 Average 6.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 1.25m Radius A trio of stone claws erupt from the ground, petrifying and inflicting Slash damage on everyone caught in the area.
Firebug
  • Foe Target: 30-40 damage (+10 vs )
  • Foe Jump Targets (x8): 30-40 damage (+10 vs )
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) 10m + 5m Jump Foe Target + 8 Jump Targets A fearsome ball of fire bounces from enemy to enemy up to eight times, causing Burn damage.
Nature's Terror
  • AoE: 10-15 damage ( vs )
  • AoE: If successful: Terrified for 3 seconds ( vs )
 Fast 30.0 seconds 1.25m Radius from Caster Calls upon the dreadful strength of the storm, granting an electrical aura that deals Shock damage to and Terrifies anyone nearby.
Plague of Insects
  • Foe AoE: 88 Raw damage over time
  • Foe AoE: -20 Concentration
  • Foe AoE: Sickened
 Average 30.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 15m 5.0m Radius Summons an enormous infestation of venomous insects, dealing Raw damage over time, reducing Concentration and Sickening anyone in the area of effect.
Relentless Storm
  • Foe AoE: 9-12 damage (+10 vs )
  • Foe AoE: If successful: Stunned for 2 seconds (+10 vs )
 Average 15.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 5.0m Radius from Caster Brings a deadly storm into being, striking enemies frequently in the area of effect with lightning bolts that Stun and deal Shock damage.
Wall of Thorns
  • Hazard AoE: 4-6 damage
  • Hazard AoE: Weakened for 5 seconds
 Average 30.0 seconds 0.5 sec (Average) 12m 20m Wall Creates a thick wall of gnarled thorns, inflicting Pierce damage. It also applies a Poison effect that inflicts a Weakened state, failing a Fortitude check.

6th level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Conjure Greater Blight
  • Summon 1 of: Greater Rain Blight, Greater Wind Blight, Greater Earth Blight, Greater Flame Blight
 Slow 30.0 seconds 6m Gathers an immense quantity of stray soul fragments and coalesces them into a Greater Flame, Wind, Rain, or Earth Blight.
Garden of Life
  • Friendly AoE: +20 Endurance
 Average 15m Target + 5.0m Radius Hastens Berath's Wheel, causing rejuvenating plants to spring up in the corpses of the fallen and heal allies in the area of effect.
Rot Skulls
  • Summon Weapon: Rot Skull
 Fast 30.0 seconds Caster Creates rotting, disease-riddled skulls in hand of the caster, acting as a ranged weapon that cause Crush damage and apply a Disease-based Corrode area of effect that does damage over time.
Sunlance
  • Target: 46-58 damage
  • Target: +50% damage
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) 20m Calls down a powerful lance of sunlight from the sky, inflicting Pierce and Burn damage upon a single target.
Venombloom
  • Foe AoE: 10-15 Raw damage
  • Foe AoE: If successful: Weakened, Frightened for 3 seconds
 Average 15.0 seconds 10m Poison: 2.5m Radius Grows a beautiful but panic-inducing field of poisonous flowers that Frighten, Weaken and deal Raw Damage over time to those in the area of effect.

7th level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Call to the Primordials [WM1]
  • Summon 2-3 of: Black Ooze, Frost Ooze, Swamp Slime
 Slow 35.0 seconds 6m The druid pulls the raw spiritual energy of nature together into the physical forms of 2 or 3 random oozes and slimes.
Nature's Bounty (spell) [WM1]
  • Allied AoE: Fill Quick Items with Nature's Bounty
 Average 5.0m Radius from Caster All unoccupied quick slots in the party are filled with wondrous nectar that can be consumed for combat bonuses and healing.
Weather the Storm [WM1]
  • Friendly AoE: +15 vs
Average 10.0 seconds 5m 2.5m Radius The druid casts a powerful aegis against the forces of nature over his or her allies, granting large resistance to Burn, Corrode, Freeze, and Shock damage.

8th level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Effects Speed Duration Interrupt Range Area Description
Avenging Storm [WM2]
  • Foe hitting Caster: 20-30 damage ( vs )
  • Foe hitting Caster: If successful: Stunned for 0.8 second ( vs )
  • Foe hit by Caster: 20-30 damage ( vs )
  • Foe hit by Caster: If successful: Stunned for 0.8 second ( vs )
 Fast 20.0 seconds The druid makes himself or herself a conduit for the power of a storm. Any target they strike in melee, or who strikes them in melee, is attacked by a bolt of lightning.
Fire Stag [WM2]
  • Summon: Stag
 Slow 35.0 seconds 6m The druid coalesces tiny nature spirits into the form of a great stag made of flame. The stag leaves a trail of fire wherever it runs. The spirits inside can be made to disperse violently, destroying the stag but doing Fire or Pierce damage to anyone caught in the blast.
Tornado [WM2]
  • Target: 45-55 damage, Prone
  • On bounce: -50% damage
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) Length: 30m, bounces up to 3 times Conjures a destructive vortex of wind that can launch people into the air, knock victims Prone, and inflict Crush damage on everyone it touches.
