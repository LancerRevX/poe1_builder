1st level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Speed Range Area Duration Effects Description
Holy Radiance Fast 5.0m Radius from Caster 15.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +15 (base)
  • Foe AoE (Vessels): 30 damage over 3 seconds
  • Foe AoE (Vessels): If successful: Frightened
 Creates a field of holy energy around the priest, Burning any vessels in the vicinity and regenerating a modest amount of Endurance for allies. The power of a priest's Holy Radiance is modified by how well his or her Reputations align with the preferred behaviors of his or her faith.
Armor of Faith Average 5m 2.5m Radius 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +4
Creates a tangible shield of faith, increasing the Damage Reduction of allies in the area of effect.
Barbs of Condemnation Average 12m 10.0 seconds
  • 20-30 damage
  • -5 All
Punishes a target for their sins, decreasing their Deflection, Fortitude, Reflex, and Will.
Blessing Average 5m 5.0m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +5
  • Friendly AoE: +10%
A call for divine favor that increases the Accuracy and damage of allies in the area of effect.
Divine Terror Average 5m 1.25m Radius 15.0 seconds
  • Foe AoE: Frightened
 Strikes fear into the hearts of the unworthy, inflicting a penalty to Accuracy on all enemies in the area of effect.
Halt Fast 10m 10.0 seconds
  • Target: Stuck
 Commands a single enemy to halt, causing them to temporarily cease all movement.
Holy Meditation Fast 10m 2.5m Radius 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +30 Concentration
  • Friendly AoE: +15 Will
 The priest clears his or her mind, spreading that clarity to nearby allies, thereby increasing their Concentration and Will Bonuses.
Prayer Against Fear Fast 5m 3.0m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: Immunity: Frightened, Terrified
 Steels the mind of allies in the area of effect against all external fears, granting immunity to the Frightened and Terrified afflictions.
Restore Minor Endurance Fast 5m 1.5m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +12
Shares a portion of the caster's divine strength, restoring some Endurance to all allies in the area of effect.
Withdraw Fast 5m 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly Target: Invisible
  • Friendly Target: +76.7 over time
  • Friendly Target: Stasis Shield
 Isolates a single target within a protective sphere, shielding them from harm while their Endurance regenerates.

Spell Speed Range Area Duration Effects Description
Consecrated Ground Average 2.5m Radius from Caster 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +9.3 per second
 Creates a long-lasting circle of healing, regenerating ally Endurance.
Divine Mark Average 2.5m 10.0 seconds
  • 40-50
  • -25
Marks a target for retribution, inflicting Burn damage and lowering Deflection.
Holy Power Average Aura Range: 1.3m 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly Aura: +3 Might

Iconic Projection Fast Length: 10m
  • Foe Target: 20 damage
  • Friendly Target: +20
Creates a slow-moving energetic projection of the priest's faith, returning Endurance to allies and inflicting Freeze damage to enemies.
Instill Doubt Average 5m 1.75m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Foe AoE: Dazed
 Taps into subconscious uncertainties, reducing the Speed of all enemies in the area of effect.
Prayer Against Infirmity Fast 5m 3.0m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: Immunity: Sickened, Weakened
 Fortifies the health of allies in the area of effect, granting immunity to the Sickened and Weakened afflictions.
Repulsing Seal Average 5m 1.25m Radius Hazard 600.0 seconds
  • Hazard AoE: Prone for 7 seconds
 Places a magical seal on the floor that sends enemies sprawling on contact, causing them to become Prone.
Restore Light Endurance Fast 5m 1.5m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +18
Shares a portion of the caster's divine strength, restoring some Endurance to all allies in the area of effect.
Suppress Affliction Average 5m 1.75m Radius 5.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: Hostile effects suspended
 Stifles detrimental conditions through sheer force of will, temporarily suspending hostile status effects on allies in the area of effect.

Circle of Protection Average 10m 5.0m Radius 10.0 seconds

Creates a large zone of divine shelter, granting allies a bonus to all Defenses.

  • Foe AoE: -15 Melee
  • Foe AoE: +15% of Crits converted to Hits
 Nagging doubt in the face of unbreakable faith consumes enemies in the area of effect, lowering their Melee Accuracy and Critical Hit range.
Dire Blessing Fast 5m 2.5m Radius 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +20% of Hits converted to Crits
 Strengthens allies' resolve in battle, increasing the probability they will land a Critical Hit.

  • Target: 30-40   (+10 vs )
  • Foe AoE: Prone (+15 vs )
 Calls down a pillar of divine judgment, inflicting Crush damage and Knockdown on enemies in the area of effect.
Prayer Against Restraint Fast 5m 3.0m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: Immunity: Hobbled, Stuck
 Frees allies in the area of effect from perceived constraints, granting immunity to the Hobbled and Stuck afflictions.
Restore Moderate Endurance Fast 5m 1.5m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +26
Shares a portion of the caster's divine strength, restoring a moderate amount of Endurance to all allies in the area of effect.

 Generates a magical seal that explodes in a burst of electrical energy when an enemy comes into contact with it, inflicting Shock damage.
Watchful Presence Average 5m 5.0m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +35 when Endurance below 20%
 Blesses allies with the aid of a sympathetic soul, releasing a healing effect when their Endurance is low.

4th level spells[edit | edit source]

Spell Speed Range Area Duration Effects Description
Barring Death's Door Fast 5m 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly Target: Prevent Death
 Prevents a severely wounded character's Health from reaching 0 when it would result in the death of the character. The character can still be knocked out through Endurance loss.
Devotions for the Faithful Average 5m 2.5m Radius 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +4 Might, +20
  • Foe AoE: -10 Might, -20
Invokes the benediction of the priest's god, imbuing allies in the area of effect with Accuracy and Might bonuses while enemies receive penalties to the same.
Litany Against Minor Afflictions [WM2] Fast 5m 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly Target: Immunity: Blinded, Hobbled, Frightened, Distracted, Dazed, Confused, Prone, Stuck, Sickened
 Grants a single ally temporary immunity to the Blinded, Confused, Dazed, Distracted, Frightened, Hobbled, Prone, Sickened, and Stuck afflictions.
Prayer Against Bewilderment Fast 5m 3.0m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: Immunity: Confused, Dazed
 Bestows alacrity upon the minds of allies in the area of effect, granting immunity to the Confused and Dazed afflictions.
Restore Major Endurance Fast 5m 1.5m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +38
Shares a generous portion of the caster's divine strength, restoring a significant amount of Endurance to all allies in the area of effect.
Searing Seal Average 10m 2.5m Radius Hazard 600.0 seconds
  • 20-35 damage ( vs )
  • If successful: Blinded for 15 seconds ( vs )
 Places a magical seal on the floor that bursts into flame when an enemy comes into contact with it, Blinding them and inflicting Burn damage.
Shining Beacon Average 5m 2.5m Radius 9.0 seconds
  • Foe AoE: -10 All
  • Foe AoE: 80 damage over time
 Conjures an intensely luminous beacon of light, causing enemies in the area of effect to take a penalty to all defenses and accrue Burn damage over time.
Triumph of the Crusaders Average 10m 1.25m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +200 per kill
 Imparts to allies the fervor of righteousness in battle. When any ally in the area of effect delivers a killing blow, he or she regains Endurance.

Spell Speed Range Area Duration Effects Description
Champion's Boon Average 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly Target: +10 Might
  • Friendly Target: +10 Perception
  • Friendly Target: +5
Grants a divine favor to an ally, imbuing him or her with a bonus to Might, Perception and all Damage Reductions.
Pillar of Holy Fire Average 10m 1.25m Radius
  • AoE: 50-60 damage
 Summons a flaming pillar of righteous anger, Burning everyone in the area of effect.
Prayer Against Imprisonment Fast 5m 3.0m Radius 20.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: Immunity: Paralyzed, Petrified
 Instills a spirit of liberation in allies in the area of effect, granting immunity to the Paralyzed and Petrified afflictions.
Restore Critical Endurance Fast 5m 1.5m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +55
Shares a substantial portion of the caster's divine strength, restoring a large amount of Endurance to all allies in the area of effect.
Revive the Fallen Average 5m 1.25m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: Revive with 50
Grants fallen friends a second chance, reviving unconscious allies in the area of effect and restoring a small amount of their Endurance.
Salvation of Time Average 10m 2.5m Radius
  • Friendly AoE: +10 seconds duration of active beneficial effects
 Beseeches the gods for more time, extending the duration of all beneficial effects on allies in the area of effect.
Shields for the Faithful Average 10m 1.75m Radius 30.0 seconds
  • Friendly AoE: +25
Conjures a powerful holy shield, granting a Deflection bonus to all allies in the area of effect.

Spell Speed Range Area Duration Effects Description
Hand of Weal and Woe [WM2] Fast 5m 10.0 seconds
  • Foe Target: 25 damage per second
  • Foe Beam: 25 damage per second
  • Friendly Target: +25 per second
  • Friendly Beam: +25 per second
 The priest calls forth a beam of light that heals allies and burns enemies caught in the ray.

  • Foe AoE: 15-25 damage
  • Foe AoE: If successful: Weakened for 3 seconds
 Emblazons the ground with the symbol of Berath, periodically causing Corrode damage to enemies and leaving them Weakened.

  • Foe AoE: 15-25 damage
  • Friendly AoE: +20 All for 3 seconds

  • Foe AoE: 15-25 damage
  • Foe AoE: If successful: Blinded for 3 seconds

  • Foe AoE: 15-25 damage ( vs )
