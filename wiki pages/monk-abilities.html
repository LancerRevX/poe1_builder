Monk abilities

From Pillars of Eternity Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search
18px This article is about monk abilities in Pillars of Eternity. For monk abilities in Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire, see Monk abilities (Deadfire).

The following is a list of monk abilities in Pillars of Eternity. For more information see the Monk class page.

List of abilities[edit | edit source]

Ability Acquirable
at level 		Activation Effects Speed Interrupt Duration Description
Transcendent Suffering 1 Passive
  • Self: +2 Unarmed
  • Self: +0 Unarmed
Monks achieve greater understanding of their bodies' capabilities through enduring the hardships of existence. Their base Unarmed Damage starts much higher than other characters and increases permanently every three levels they gain.
Swift Strikes 1 Active
  • +25% Attack Speed
 Instant 10.0 seconds The monk's hands become a frenzied blur of attacks, increasing their Attack Rate for a brief period.
Torment's Reach 1 Active
  • Foe AoE: -2 Might
  • User: Full Attack, +50% damage as
Average 20.0 seconds This ability exploits the shared bonds of universal suffering between all beings. The initial target is hit with a powerful blow that does additional Crush damage. Enemies in a cone behind the target suffer Crush damage and have their Might reduced.
Force of Anguish 3 Active
  • Foe Target: 7.0m Push ( vs )
  • Foe Target: If successful: Prone ( vs )
  • User: Primary Attack ( vs )
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) 10.0 seconds A powerful attack that knocks the target back a significant distance if the attack is successful. Target bumps others out of the way and bounces off hard surfaces like walls before ending up Prone.
Long Stride 3 Passive
  • Self: +2 Move Speed
 Intense practice and years of study enable the monk to move with confident fluidity in battle. During combat, the monk's Movement Rate is significantly increased.
Turning Wheel 3 Passive
  • Self: +5% damage per Wound
 The monk is able to channel physical pain into pure energy and redirect it at his or her attackers. While the monk has Wounds, he or she adds a proportional fire bonus to Melee Damage.
Transcendent Suffering 2 4 Passive
  • Self: +4 Unarmed
  • Self: +4 Unarmed
Monks achieve greater understanding of their bodies' capabilities through enduring the hardships of existence. Their base Unarmed Damage starts much higher than other characters and increases permanently every three levels they gain.
Clarity Of Agony 5 Active
  • -50% duration of hostile effects
 Instant 10.0 seconds Through pain the monk is able to cleanse his or her body, reducing the duration of incoming and existing hostile effects by half.
Soul Mirror 5 Passive
  • 50% chance to reflect Ranged Attacks
 Calls upon the power of the monk's psyche, reflecting half of ranged missiles targeting only the monk back to their point of origin. Only attacks resulting in a Miss have a chance to be reflected back.
Stunning Blow 5 Active
  • Target: If successful: Stunned ( vs )
  • User: Full Attack ( vs )
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) 3.0 seconds A head-targeting strike designed to disrupt enemies' ability to react. Enemies hit by a successful attack are Stunned.
Transcendent Suffering 3 7 Passive
  • Self: +6 Unarmed
  • Self: +8 Unarmed
Monks achieve greater understanding of their bodies' capabilities through enduring the hardships of existence. Their base Unarmed Damage starts much higher than other characters and increases permanently every three levels they gain.
Crucible Of Suffering 7 Passive
  • +10 Fortitude
  • +10 Reflex
  • +10 Will
 20.0 seconds The monk gains temporary insight through endurance, gaining a temporary bonus to Fortitude, Reflex, and Will whenever a hostile effect expires.
Duality of Mortal Presence 7 Passive
  • Grants Duality of Mortal Presence - Deflection
  • Grants Duality of Mortal Presence - Other Defenses
 The monk gains two modal abilities that allow him or her to switch between a bonus to Deflection and a bonus to all other defenses.
Rooting Pain 7 Passive
  • Foe AoE: 10-15 damage
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) Generates a shockwave around the monk each time he or she gains a Wound, inflicting a small amount of damage to and possibly calling an Interrupt on all enemies in the area of effect.
The Long Pain [WM2] 7 Active
  • Summon Weapon: Long Pain Fists
 Average 30.0 seconds The monk becomes capable of projecting force from his or her fists, inflicting damage at long range.
Enervating Blows 9 Passive
  • Target: Weakened on Crits
 15.0 seconds By targeting certain points on an enemy, the monk is able to inflict physical weakness. Melee Critical Hits cause the target to become Weakened.
Transcendent Suffering 4 10 Passive
  • Self: +8 Unarmed
  • Self: +12 Unarmed
Monks achieve greater understanding of their bodies' capabilities through enduring the hardships of existence. Their base Unarmed Damage starts much higher than other characters and increases permanently every three levels they gain.
Flagellant's Path 11 Active
  • Foe Target: 20-30 damage
  • Foe Target: -10 All
Average 0.5 sec (Average) 8.0 seconds In the blink of an eye, the monk streaks toward a distant target, inflicting terrible Crush damage and penalties to all defenses on anyone in his or her path.
Iron Wheel [WM1] 11 Passive
  • +1 per Wound
 The pain from the monk's wounds allows him or her to build Damage Reduction against incoming attacks.
Skyward Kick [WM2] 13 Active
  • Target: If successful: Prone ( vs )
  • User: Primary Attack ( vs )
 Average 0.5 sec (Average) 3.0 seconds The monk launches a target into the air, slamming them back down to the ground and leaving them Prone.
The Dichotomous Soul [WM1] 13 Active
  • Summon: Monk Duplicate
 Average 15.0 seconds The monk meditates briefly on his or her pain, summoning two physical manifestations of suffering, one made of flame, the other of ice.
Resonant Touch [WM2] 15 Active
  • AoE: 10 Raw damage per Resonance
  • Foe Target: Resonance when dealt at least 10
Average The monk's strikes generate a sympathetic resonance between the monk and his or her target. Successive strikes increase the intensity of the resonance. At a time of the monk's choosing, the monk can cause the resonance to inflict devastating Raw damage to anyone currently carrying them.
